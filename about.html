About Kynyx Solutions

We Build Software That
Solves Real Problems

Kynyx Solutions is a software and digital consulting firm based in Patna, Bihar. We work with startups and established businesses to design and build products that create real impact — not just pretty interfaces.

Our Story

Started Small. Stayed Focused.

Kynyx Solutions was founded by a small group of developers and designers who were tired of seeing businesses get burned by agencies that overpromised and underdelivered.

We started with one belief: good software comes from understanding the business first, not jumping straight into code. That mindset shapes everything we do — from the first discovery call to post-launch support.

We've since grown into a full-service digital team with expertise across web, mobile, cloud, and AI — while keeping the tight-knit, accountable team culture that made us different from day one.

Our Mission

To deliver honest, high-quality software solutions that help Indian businesses compete in the digital economy — without unnecessary complexity or inflated costs.

Our Vision

To become the go-to technology partner for growth-stage businesses in India — known for reliability, quality, and a team that genuinely understands business.

FAQ

Common Questions

Things people usually ask before working with us.

How much does a project typically cost?

+

A basic website starts around ₹25,000, while a custom web application typically ranges from ₹80,000 to ₹3,00,000+. We provide a detailed quote after a free discovery call.

How long does development take?

+

A landing page or brochure site takes 2–3 weeks. A web application with custom features usually takes 6–14 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline after scoping.

Do you work with clients outside Patna / Bihar?

+

Yes — we work fully remote with clients across India. All coordination via video calls, Slack, and shared project boards.

What happens after launch?

+

Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support. For ongoing maintenance, we offer monthly retainer plans starting at ₹8,000/month.

Do you sign NDAs?

+

Yes. We're happy to sign an NDA before any technical discussions. We take client confidentiality seriously.
